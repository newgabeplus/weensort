Sorter for Ween songs. Pick your sources, and hit the Start button! Some options have additional info when hovered over.

Click on the song you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or haven't heard them.

Depending on how many sources you pick, you'll be here a while. With all albums enabled this sorter will take over an hour, but single albums only take a few minutes at the most.

I'd personally recommend unchecking all live albums and demo tapes unless you're a hardcore Ween fan!



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
